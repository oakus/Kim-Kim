Our Story

Welcome

We, at Kim Kim have been serving high quality Hakka food in the GTA for the last 10 years. This incredible cuisine originates from China, but has gradually been influenced by Indian tastes, which has brought about an amazing combination of flavours which we enjoy today. The recipe from Mei Zhou (China) was brought to India by the Hakka Chinese settlement in Calcutta (now Kolkata) in east India over 100 years ago. The recipes at Kim Kim are combined with ingredients from the sub-continent to give a fresh new thrilling treat for the taste buds. The success and growing popularity of the ethnic Hakka food in Toronto has made us a popular destination among food lovers. By constantly striving for excellence and perfection, we are confident that our customers will enjoy our excellent food and service! Drop by for dine in or call us for takeout today!
